Our Minister

Name: Mr. Tayseer Manizel Naeimi

Place and date of birth: arbod-1955

Marital status: Married

Present post: minister of Education

Education :-
Ph. D. in pedagogical Psychology, University of Pittsburgh /America, 1986 Master of education (psychological guidance), University of Yarmouk 1981 B. A., University of Jordan, 1977


The most important positions he's ever held. :-
1977-1983 high school teacher, arbad city
1981-1983 part - time lecturer-University of Yarmouk
1984-1986 teaching assistant, Department of Educational Psychology - University of Pittsburgh Pennsylvania - USA
1986-1992 Assistant Professor / Faculty of Educational Sciences-Mota University
1992-1998 Associate Professor / Department of Psychology - University
1996-1997 Associate Professor /Department of Psychology - University of United Arab Emirates
1997-2004 deputy chairman of the National Centre for Human Resources Development and stadium problems in Balqa applied University
2002-2004 pedagogical expert in the Development Coordination Unit, Ministry of Education
2001 professor at Balqa applied university / deputy head of the National Center for Human Resources Development
2004-2007 Secretary General of the Ministry of education for educational and technical
2007-2009 Minister of Education
Senior consultant for many international institutions in matters of education development such as the World Bank, UNESCO, UNICEF UNDP.
Member of the 24th Senate.
2011-2012 Minister of Education.
