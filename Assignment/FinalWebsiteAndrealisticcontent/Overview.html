Overview

This system was created esspecially for the parents; because all parents worry about their children and want to know how are they preforming in School. As the name implaies, this system will give you access to your children life at school, so you can monitor his/her progress. you can monitor their marks, behavior, activities that they are enrolling in, Adcademic and Weekly schedule (Except for the Kindergarten and Preschool where they on have the Weekly schedule), the feedback that comes from their teachers, and finally and only for High Schoolars, Universites they applied for. so you just have to enter your Parent ID and password, givin to you from the school you child is registered in and login to your dashboard.
