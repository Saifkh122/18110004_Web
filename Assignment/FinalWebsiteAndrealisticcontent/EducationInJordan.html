Education In Jordan

We broadcast the philosophy of education in the kingdom of Jordan's Constitution, Arab-Islamic culture and principles of the Great Arab Revolt and trade Jordanian national and is this philosophy in the following grounds:-
Intellectual foundations: Faith in God. Faith in the ideals of the Arab nation. Islam is an intellectual and behavioural system that respects the human person, elevates the status of the mind and encourages science, work and creation. Islam value system that integrated the values and principles of good which constitute the conscience of the individual and the community. The relationship between Islam and Arabism is organic.
National, national and human foundations: The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan Arab state system of government a parliamentary hereditary monarchy and where God and home and. Jordan is part of the Arab nation and the Jordanian people are an integral part of the Arab and Islamic nation. The Jordanian people are an integrated unit with no place for racial, regional, sectarian, clan or family intolerance. Arabic is a cornerstone of the existence of the Arab nation and a factor in its unity and Renaissance. The Great Arab revolution reflects the aspiration of the Arab nation and its aspirations for independence, liberation, unity and progress. To uphold the Arab status of Palestine and all the usurped parts of the Arab world and to seek to recover it. The Palestinian question is the fate of the people of Jordan, and the Zionist aggression on the Palestinian challenge political, military and civilization of the Arab-Islamic in general and Jordan in particular. The Arab nation is a historical fact well established, Arab unity is a vital necessity for its existence and advancement. Balance between National, national and Islamic character on the one hand and openness to global cultures on the other. Adapt to the changes of the age and provide self-capacity to meet its requirements. International understanding based on justice, equality and freedom. Active participation in and development of global civilization.
Social foundations: Jordanians are equal in rights and duties, political, social, economic and the extent of their giving to their community or not. Respect for the freedom and dignity of the individual. Social justice, balancing the needs of the individual with those of society, the cooperation and solidarity of the individual in the public interest and the assumption of individual and social responsibility. Society is made up of individuals who are organized in the national and national interest. Political and social participation in a democratic system is an individual's right and duty to his or her society. Education is a social necessity and education is a right of all, according to their abilities and abilities.
General objectives: - Derive the general objectives for development in the kingdom of the philosophy of Education consists in the formation of the citizen believer in God come home to my homeland, the nation, the museum, my virtues state of humanity developing in various aspects of personal physical, mental and spiritual and emotional and social-so that the student becomes in the end stages of the education materials we are able to:
Use Arabic for self-expression and easy communication with others. Absorption conscious of the facts, concepts and relationships relating to the natural environment, geography, population, social and cultural rights locally and globally and use them effectively in public life. Understanding the elements of heritage and drawing lessons for understanding and developing the present. Accommodating Islam doctrine and group discrimination, conscious of the value of the trends. Openness to the values and good attitudes of human cultures. Mathematical thinking and use numerical systems and mathematical relations in the scientific and public life. Assimilate facts, concepts, principles and theories and deal with them and use them in the interpretation of the phenomena of the universe and harnessing them to serve human problem solving by providing reasons for his happiness. Conscious assimilation of technology and the acquisition of skills in dealing with it, producing, developing and harnessing it to serve society. Collection, storage, recall, processing, production and use of information to explain phenomena, anticipate different possibilities of events and take decisions in various areas. Objective critical thinking and scientific methods of observation, research and problem-solving. To meet the requirements of work and self-reliance by acquiring General and specialized professional skills. Internalization of health norms, the practice of related habits and sporting activity to achieve balanced physical growth. Taste the aesthetic aspects of different arts and life. Upholding the rights and responsibilities of citizenship. Islamic, national and national pride. To invest private capacities and free time in the development of knowledge, creativity, innovation, entrepreneurship and leisure. Human dignity and the formation of positive values and trends towards oneself and others, work and social progress.democratic principles are represented in individual and social behaviour. Personal adaptation, the acquisition of social and ethical codes of conduct and their representation in dealing with others and life variables
Principles of educational policy: The principles of educational policy are as follows::- To direct the educational system to be more tailored and balanced to the needs of the individual and society. Provide opportunity to achieve the principle of education for sustainable investment styles parallel translation in coordination with the competent authorities. Emphasize the importance of political education in the educational system and to consolidate the principles of participation, justice, democracy, and practice. Guide the process of educational guidance develops in the person and The Citizen the ability to transform people and the initiative and creativity, and dialogue positive and promoting values derived from Arab and Islamic civilization and humanity. The establishment of a scientific curriculum in the educational system in order to plan, implement, evaluate and develop research, evaluation and monitoring systems. Expansion of teaching methods in educational institutions to include special education programmes, gifted children and persons with special needs. Emphasize the concept of comprehensive expertise, including professional and technological expertise. Emphasize that education is a message and a profession with moral and professional rules. Directing the education system to ensure the achievement of central planning, General, decentralization in management. Recognition of the complex scientific and social role of building human society. Stress the importance of military education and environmental culture.
